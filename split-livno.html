OVERVIEW

AN ADVENTURE BEYOND THE COAST

This journey is created for you if you crave movement, meaning, and memorable landscapes.

You begin on the sunlit Adriatic in Split and gradually travel inland toward the wild highlands of Bosnia, experiencing a powerful contrast of sea, stone, forest, and open plateau.

Along the way, you zip above canyons, descend waterfalls, hike panoramic mountain trails, hunt for truffles with local experts, sail privately to Hvar, and encounter wild horses roaming freely across vast horizons.

Every day invites you to explore. Every evening welcomes you back to refined comfort.

This is adventure...elevated.

JOURNEY HIGHLIGHTS

Soar above Cetina Canyon on thrilling zip lines

Dive, slide, and abseil waterfalls in wild canyon

Hike Biokovo's breathtaking Adriatic views

Hunt truffles with local experts and dogs

Spot wild horses roaming Bosnian plateaus

Cross Mostar's iconic bridge and sip traditional coffee

Sail privately to Hvar with secret swim stops

Mix adventure with stylish boutique comfort

Welcome to Split - Adriatic Ease

Arrive in Split and transfer privately to Heritage Hotel Fermai 4*, an elegant boutique base in the heart of the city.

Relax with a stroll through the historic streets, enjoy the sea breeze along the promenade, or simply unwind after your journey and embrace the Mediterranean rhythm.

DAY 2

Fly Above the Canyon & Taste Dalmatian Tradition

Travel along the coast to Omiš, where mountains rise dramatically above the Cetina River. Glide across eight zip lines suspended high above the canyon, feeling the thrill as the river and cliffs stretch out below you - a panoramic adventure you won't forget.

Afterwards, visit a charming family home to experience Dalmatian culinary tradition. Join host Snježana in preparing Soparnik, a classic local dish of Swiss chard, garlic, onion, and olive oil wrapped in thin pastry. Learn the techniques passed down through generations, then sit down to enjoy the dish you've prepared alongside local prosciutto, cheese, and wine. Return to Split for a relaxed evening at Heritage Hotel Fermai 4*, carrying memories of adrenaline and authentic Dalmatian flavours.

Hiking Biokovo's Panoramic Trails

DAY 5

Extreme Canyoning - A Thrilling Journey Through the Cetina Canyon

Descend into the spectacular canyon of the Cetina River for a full adrenaline experience in one of Dalmatia's most dramatic natural settings. Accompanied by professional guides and equipped with all necessary safety gear, you will make your way through crystal-clear emerald pools, navigate narrow passages carved by water over centuries, and slide down smooth natural rock formations shaped by the river. The highlight of the adventure is abseiling beside a powerful waterfall, surrounded by towering cliffs rising up to 180 metres above you - an unforgettable moment that combines thrill and breathtaking scenery. After this exhilarating day immersed in nature, return to your hotel in Split for a well-deserved evening of relaxation.

DAY 6

Into Bosnia - Wild Horses & Wide Horizons

After the check-out, travel inland toward Bosnia, where the landscape becomes vast and untamed. Enjoy the comfort and luxury of your private driver and vehicle throughout your journey. Check in at A Frame Dinara, a unique elevated retreat overlooking Buško Lake. Explore the Kruzi plateau, watching wild horses roam freely across open grasslands, a cinematic encounter with nature.

Savour a traditional outdoor lunch featuring homemade pies like burek, zeljanica, and krumpiruša, hearty goulash, barbecue, and local wines, brandies, and natural juices - a true taste of the region.

Mostar & Return to the Adriatic

Experience a traditional Bosnian coffee ritual and savour local flavours before returning to Split. Check in at the elegant seafront Hotel Ambasador Split 5* for your final Adriatic evenings.

Private Boat Escape to Hvar & Pakleni Islands

Cruise aboard a private luxury boat from Hotel Ambasador Split to Hvar Island, enjoying stunning Adriatic views. Stop to swim at the crystal-clear coves of the Pakleni Islands, then continue to Hvar for a short walking tour. Enjoy a relaxed lunch at a local restaurant with fresh Mediterranean flavors. Visit a traditional stone konoba, preserved as it was decades ago, where you'll taste local wine and homemade orahovac while learning about island life. Return to Split by private boat for your final night at Hotel Ambasador Split.
